What is the Ruby City MATSUE Project?

With the theme of OSS and 'Ruby', aiming to create a new regional brand as 'the city of Ruby' With the keyword of 'Ruby' as a keyword, we can stop by where we can stop by and feel free to exchange technology and information We aim to play a role as an exchange center for human resources and information and a base for business matching. We will provide regional talent to the community from Ruby Human Resource Development for students and provide high quality Ruby Human Resource Development Environment through industry-university-government collaboration. In addition, by promoting the IT industry and promoting the location of the company, we will secure a place of employment of cultivated personnel.
