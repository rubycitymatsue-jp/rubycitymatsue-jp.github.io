MATSUE OPEN SOURCE LAB

About open source laboratory

Matsue open source lab 'is set up in front of JR Matsue station which is the central city area. Based on 'Matsue open source laboratory', companies, engineers, researchers, students and users involved in open source software deepen exchanges to improve technology and competitiveness and foster excellent human resources, We are working on revitalizing industry by cultivating the market and creating a regional brand (Ruby City MATSUE). Various study sessions and events are held based on this open source lab, regularly open source study group, open source salon is held. You can use it free of charge as a meeting place, meeting place and training place on OSS.

About usage

How to use

As a general rule, you can use it freely within the opening hours (no application required).

In the following cases, 'Application for occupation' will be required at [Matsue Industry Support Center] one week in advance.

  • In case you need to monopolize and use at the holding of workshops etc. on weekdays.
  • In the case where it is used after extension of regular opening hours (maximum until 10:00 pm) before 9:30 am on weekdays and after 7 pm.
  • Saturdays-and-Sundays congratulation, when used for year-end and New Year holidays (closed holiday).

Purpose of use

Exchange of technology and information on open source software, human resource development, etc.

Example: Meeting, conference, consultation, workshop, lecture, negotiation, etc on open source software.

The opening and closing of keys for holidays and extension time will be done at the Matsue Telesa office (main building 2F).
