RubyWorld Conference

It is an international conference of Ruby to be held every year in Matsue City, Shimane Prefecture. Through various sessions introducing information on the latest technology, Ruby aims to spread to more areas, domestic and foreign speakers will speak.

Official Page

Ruby Prize

In order to appreciate those who newly participated in Ruby community activities, to promote the spread and use of Ruby, in 2013 the 'Ruby Prize' Ruby community 'Rookie Award' was founded. The Ruby Prize Executive Committee of three members, Ruby Association, Japan Ruby Association, Matsue City, is holding it. One million yen will be awarded as a prize to the winner of RubyPrize.

Matsue Open Source Business Plan Contest

Shimane OSS Council and Matsue City hold annual meetings. This contest is not only in the IT industry but also provides open source information widely to people in a wide range of fields and commercializes a new business plan utilizing open source by taking ideas from plans to utilize open source It is aimed at leading to entrepreneurialization. Through this contest, Matsue's presence as a sacred place for utilization of Ruby and OSS will be raised, which will be an opportunity for matching between the application plan and the award plan and the company, so that the human network etc. in this contest will be utilized and concrete We expect to be a move towards business development.
